## How to Use

1. **Click the purple "Fill Sample Data" button** in the top-right corner of the page
2. **All fields populate instantly** with professional example data
3. **Scroll through the form** to see examples of:
   - Proper formatting in work experience
   - How to structure education entries
   - Skill categorization
   - Project descriptions
4. **Edit any field** to replace with your own information
5. **Click "Preview PDF"** to see the formatted resume
6. **Click "Download PDF"** to save

## Customization Tips

After filling sample data:

### Replace Personal Info First
- Change name, email, phone, address
- Update LinkedIn and portfolio URLs
- Rewrite professional summary

### Modify Work Experience
- Keep the formatting style (bold for emphasis, bullets for achievements)
- Replace company names, titles, and dates
- Update descriptions with your actual accomplishments
- Keep the metrics-focused approach (percentages, numbers, impact)

### Update Education
- Add/remove entries as needed
- Replace with your actual degrees and institutions
- Update dates and GPA

### Customize Skills
- Keep the category structure
- Replace with your actual skills
- Add/remove categories as needed

### Edit Projects
- Replace with your actual projects

- Focus on impact and results
